Cloud quota and BYOK

Free local playback, clipping, and local AI subtitles do not use cloud minutes. Pro includes 600 minutes each month for cloud context correction and translation.

After the included quota, connect your own supported AI provider API key through BYOK. Charges from that provider are billed separately to you by the provider.

FAQ

Pricing questions

Can I use YOMUVIA for free?

Yes. Free costs ¥0 and includes local playback, organization, A-B repeat, quick clipping, and local AI subtitles.

How much is YOMUVIA Pro?

Pro is ¥1,480 per month or ¥14,800 per year. The seven-day free trial includes 30 cloud minutes.

What happens after I use 600 cloud minutes?

Pro includes 600 cloud minutes each month. After the quota, you can continue with your own supported API key through BYOK.
