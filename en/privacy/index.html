Privacy

Your videos stay local by default.

Kagura Player separates local playback and recognition from optional network services. This pre-release policy describes the implemented boundaries and launch requirements.

Local data

Videos are played from your storage. Favorites, playlists, settings, subtitle caches, and correction memories are stored on your PC. Local Whisper recognition does not upload the video.

Optional cloud AI

When cloud correction or translation is configured and used, recognized text, the video filename, and limited context may be sent to the configured AI provider. The video file itself is not sent by this flow.

Licensing and updates

Pro activation and refresh send an installation identifier and purchase-session information to the licensing service. Update checks send the installed version and channel to the configured update endpoint. Production endpoints are not active in the preview build.

Logs

Application logs are written locally for troubleshooting. There is no automatic crash-upload pipeline in the current build.

Control and deletion

You can remove app data through the uninstall flow or delete the Kagura Player data folder. Final support and privacy contact details will be published before paid launch.
