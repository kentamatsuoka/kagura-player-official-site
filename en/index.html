FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is YOMUVIA?

YOMUVIA is a Windows video player that combines local AI subtitles and translation, A-B clipping, a clip library, and video joining in one workflow.

What can I use now?

Playback, favorites, folders, A-B repeat, quick A-B clipping, and background export are implemented.

Are AI subtitles and translation available?

They are implemented. Recognition runs locally, while cloud correction and translation are used only when configured.

How much does YOMUVIA cost?

Free is ¥0. Pro is ¥1,480 per month or ¥14,800 per year, with a seven-day trial, 30 trial cloud minutes, 600 monthly cloud minutes, and BYOK beyond quota.

Can YOMUVIA join multiple clips?

Yes. Add clips from the library, drag them into order, preview them, and join compatible media into one output.
