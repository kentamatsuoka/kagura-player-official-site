# Content Guide

## Product Voice

Clear, fast, practical. Avoid overclaiming.

## Core Phrase

```text
見る。残す。すぐ切り抜く。
```

## Completed vs Planned

Completed/initial:

- Favorites.
- Folders.
- A-B repeat.
- Quick clipping from A-B range.
- Background save.

Planned:

- Local AI subtitles.
- Subtitle-burned clips.
- Subtitle styling and timing controls.

Never present planned Pro features as already available.
